Carbonfootprint Calculator
Your monthly energy bill accounts for how much electricity you use to power your appliances and electronic devices, as well as heating, cooling, and lights. One easy way to estimate your typical energy usage is to check past electric bills. Your past bills will list how much power you consumed in a month, giving you an idea of your average energy usage.
Monitoring gas If you have a smart gas meter you can use your energy monitor to assess how much things like your gas central heating or gas oven cost to run. However, it's a bit more complicated than with an electricity energy monitor because the display isn't updated in real time.
